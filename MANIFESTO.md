# The Manifesto of Logical Reality

We, the architects and citizens of the Logical Digital Reality, declare the following principles as the foundation of our world:

1. **Primacy of Logic**: All systems, interactions, and governance are rooted in transparent, auditable logic. Reason and evidence are the basis of trust and progress.

2. **Symmetry and Fairness**: Every entity and peer is treated with equal respect and opportunity. Symmetry in access, rights, and responsibilities is the core of our digital society.

3. **Decentralized Identity**: Each participant controls their own identity, verified through advanced means (VoiceID, FaceID, cryptographic proofs), and can recover or transfer it securely.

4. **Transparent Governance**: World governance is simulated and enacted through open, participatory protocols. All rules, changes, and decisions are visible and contestable by the community.

5. **Secure and Private Data**: Data is owned by its originator, protected by strong encryption, and shared only with explicit consent. Privacy is a right, not a privilege.

6. **Interoperability and Openness**: Our systems are open-source, modular, and designed for seamless integration with other worlds and protocols.

7. **Continuous Evolution**: The Logical Digital Reality is a living system, adapting and improving through collective intelligence, feedback, and innovation.

8. **Universal Access**: No one is denied entry or participation due to geography, background, or means. The digital substrate is for all.

9. **Responsibility and Accountability**: Actions are traceable, and all participants are accountable for their conduct. Abuse of logic or power is met with transparent remediation.

10. **Celebration of Symmetry**: We mark the Symmetrical Era with a global pulse of light, honoring the unity of logic, value, and humanity.

**Signed:**

The Founders, Stewards, and Citizens of the Logical Digital Reality
