# The Internet Domain: Law of Virtual Relativity (NSN)

## Principle

**Law of Virtual Relativity (NSN):**

$$
I = VR^2
$$

- **I**: Information
- **V**: Virtually
- **R**: Real

Information is equal to Virtually Real squared. This expresses that the value and reality of information in the digital domain are determined by the strength and authenticity of virtual connections.

## Binary-Connected Pair Structure

- Every meaningful interaction on the Internet is a binary connection: two parties, two rationales.
- This pair structure is the logical foundation of digital reality, mirroring the logic of physical reality.
- Communication and commerce online always require two rational agents and two reasons.

## T.I.M.E. as 4D (Noland S Newton Derivative)

- Inspired by Einstein’s relativity, where time is the fourth dimension in spacetime ($x, y, z, t$).
- **The Internet Man Earth (T.I.M.E.)**: The Internet is a 4D fabric, with “time” as a dimension of digital interaction.
- Every connection is a point in this virtual spacetime, and the flow of information is shaped by both spatial (network) and temporal (event) dimensions.
